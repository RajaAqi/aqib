## üéØ Template Use Cases

### 1. **Classic Professional**
**Best For:** Banking, Finance, Law, Government, Traditional Corporations

**Strengths:**
- Universally accepted format
- Easy to read for ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems)
- Traditional recruiters prefer it
- Professional and formal

**Weaknesses:**
- May appear outdated to tech companies
- Limited visual appeal
- Standard/common format

**Sample Roles:**
- Financial Analyst
- Legal Counsel
- Corporate Manager
- Government Officer

---

### 2. **Modern Sidebar**
**Best For:** Tech, Startups, Creative, Design, Product Management

**Strengths:**
- Eye-catching and memorable
- Great for tech companies
- Modern design trends
- Efficient use of space

**Weaknesses:**
- May not be ATS-friendly
- Requires careful PDF conversion
- Some recruiters prefer traditional

**Sample Roles:**
- Software Engineer
- UX Designer
- Product Manager
- Data Scientist

---

### 3. **Two-Column**
**Best For:** Highly Competitive Fields, Consulting, Senior Positions

**Strengths:**
- Professional and balanced
- Efficient information organization
- Stands out from single-column
- Balanced left/right visual weight

**Weaknesses:**
- May be harder to scan
- Requires more careful formatting
- Print layout can be tricky

**Sample Roles:**
- Management Consultant
- Senior Engineer
- Project Manager
- Business Analyst

---

### 4. **Minimalist**
**Best For:** Creative, Design, Academia, Prestigious Roles

**Strengths:**
- Clean and elegant
- Emphasizes content over design
- Sophisticated appearance
- Easy to read

**Weaknesses:**
- Requires strong content
- May appear empty if sparse
- Limited visual differentiation

**Sample Roles:**
- Graphic Designer
- UX Writer
- Academic Researcher
- Executive Role

---

### 5. **Dark Modern**
**Best For:** Tech Startups, Creative Tech, Design, Modern Companies

**Strengths:**
- Highly memorable
- Modern and trendy
- Great visual hierarchy
- Perfect for tech industry

**Weaknesses:**
- Not ATS-friendly without conversion
- May not print well in B&W
- Too bold for conservative industries
- Requires PDF conversion for safety

**Sample Roles:**
- Full Stack Developer
- AI/ML Engineer
- Startup Founder
- Tech Lead

## üöÄ Export Recommendations

**For Online Submission:**
- Export as PDF
- Use Chrome/Edge browser
- Verify colors and formatting
- Check file size (under 5MB ideal)

**For Email:**
- Classic or Minimalist: Plain PDF fine
- Modern/Dark: Color PDF recommended

**For Printing:**
- Classic: Any printer, B&W OK
- Modern: Color printer recommended
- Two-Column: Standard printer OK
- Minimalist: Any printer, B&W OK
- Dark: Color printer recommended

**For ATS Systems:**
- Classic Professional: Most compatible
- Two-Column: Generally compatible
- Others: May need conversion
